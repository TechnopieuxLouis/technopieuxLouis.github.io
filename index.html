What is TMP?

A Revolution in Building Foundations

Techno Metal Post offers a modern, reliable alternative to traditional concrete foundations. Our galvanised steel helical piles are precisely screwed into the ground to the correct depth, providing solid support and long-lasting stability for any type of structure.

Our Foundation System

Our foundation system uses helical piling technology, not ground screws. Our helical piles benefit from a BBA certification and are approved by Registered Building Control Approvers and Local Authorities Building Controls.

Helical Pile Foundations

We design, manufacture and install high-performance helical piles made from premium North American and European structural steel. Unlike standard ground screws, our piles are engineered, tested, and installed using proprietary hydraulic machinery operated by certified installers across the UK.

Photo: Pile close-up

Helical Pile Installation

Every project deserves a strong, dependable foundation. All our installations are carried out by certified Techno Metal Post professionals. During installation, torque is precisely measured to calculate the exact load capacity of each pile, ensuring your structure is safely supported according to on-site soil conditions.

Photo: Installer at work

Different Pile Diameters

Every project is unique. Our certified installers select the right pile size based on the required load capacity and soil conditions. Smaller piles can support up to 2,700 kg, while our robust P5 model can withstand approximately 22,700 kg — ensuring strength, stability and complete peace of mind.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a soil survey?

The need for a soil survey will depend on the planning authority in your area. Some smaller residential projects may not require one. Techno Metal Post does not provide full soil surveys, but we can carry out a probe test to assess ground conditions and determine the required depth to achieve proper bearing capacity.
