What is TMP?

A Revolution in Building Foundations

Techno Metal Post offers a modern, reliable alternative to traditional concrete foundations. Our galvanised steel helical piles are precisely screwed into the ground to the correct depth, providing solid support and long‑lasting stability for any type of structure.

Our foundation system

Our foundation system uses helical piling technology, not ground screws. our helical piles benefits from a BBA certification and are approved by Registered Building Control Approvers and Local Authorities Building Controls.

Helical Pile Foundations

We design, manufacture and install high‑performance helical piles made from premium North American and European structural steel. Our helical piles are engineered, tested, and installed using proprietary hydraulic machinery operated by certified installers across the UK.

Helical Pile Installation

Every project deserves a strong, dependable foundation. That’s why all our installations are carried out by certified Techno Metal Post professionals. During installation, torque is precisely measured to calculate the exact load capacity of each pile, ensuring your structure is safely supported according to on site soil conditions and stuctural engineers requirement.

Different pile diameters

Every project is unique. Our certified installers supported by our in-house engineering team select the right pile size based on the required load capacity and soil conditions. Our range of piles can support from 0 to 15 tons, ensuring strength, stability and complete peace of mind whatever your project.

Built for the United Kingdom

Whatever the soil conditions, from rocky ground to water‑logged clay, Techno Metal Post has the right pile for your project. Quick to install, eco‑friendly, and ready to build on immediately, our helical foundations are the smarter choice for any building project (garden rooms, extensions and refurbishment, new build, boardwalks, underpinning etc.).

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a soil survey?

The need for a soil survey will depend on the planning authority in your area. Some smaller residential projects may not require one. Techno Metal Post installers can carry out on site torque reading test to assess ground condition. A torque reading test remove the risk of variations or orverruns for your project.

How much do helical piles cost?

The cost of a pile depends on the type of project and the depth required to achieve a solid, load‑bearing foundation. When considering the time and cost of excavation, spoil removal and concrete curing, helical piles are most often economical than traditional concrete foundations. Contact us and we’ll be delighted to review your project and provide a budget estimate.

Why choose a helical pile foundation instead of concrete?

Using helical piles saves both time and money, there’s no need for excavation, no concrete curing time, no spoil removal and no post‑installation adjustments. Techno Metal Posts provide ready‑to‑build foundations that ensure long‑term stability, heave protection and help prevent future costs related to soil movement.

How long does installation take?

Techno Metal Post installation is exceptionally quick and precise. For example, we can install the foundations for a 250 m² double‑storey house in just a few days. The installation time varies depending on the type of project, soil conditions, number of piles and site accessibility.

Can helical piles be installed in rocky soil conditions?

Yes. Our installation equipment can drive piles through soil containing small to moderate‑sized rocks. Our machines generate sufficient torque for the piles to shift or cut through these obstacles. In cases of shallow bedrock or larger rocks, a specialised anchoring system can be used.

What do I need to provide to get a quote?

Techno Metal Post provides a budget estimate based on preliminary ground floor plans. As your project progresses, with loads from your structural engineers, possibly with an independent soil survey or with a Techno Metal Post torque reading test, Techno Metal Post provides a firm quote.
