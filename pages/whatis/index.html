How does it work ?

The Techno Metal Post is a metal pile used as a foundation and works like a screw. It consists of a tube, a helical shaft at the bottom, and a plate at the top. The helix serves two purposes: it allows the pile to be screwed into the ground and provides load-bearing support. The top plate, which comes in several standard sizes, is precisely positioned at the required height and supports the load-bearing parts of the structure.
The Techno Metal Post is available in various tube sizes and helix diameters.
