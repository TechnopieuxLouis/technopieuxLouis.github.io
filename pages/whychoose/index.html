Why choose Techno Metal Post ?


Simple, quick & affordable

Our Techno Metal Posts are installed by our certified installers. Thanks to our machines, the Techno Metal Posts can be installed quickly and precisely. Unlike traditional foundations, our fast, non-invasive solution allows the piles to be screwed directly into the ground, without any major excavation. This saves you precious time, and means that your project can progress smoothly, free from the usual constraints of concrete-based foundations.


No excavation & easy access to tight spaces

Our Techno Metal Posts ensure a secure anchorage in any type of soil, without the need for major excavation or the use of concrete. This eco-friendly solution is suitable for residential buildings, industrial structures, and a wide range of other construction projects.


Low footprint

Our technology is notable for its low environmental impact. Unlike traditional methods that require heavy excavation and extensive use of concrete, our solution allows piles to be installed with minimal disruption. Adapted to natural constraints, our technology blends seamlessly into its environment, preserving ecological balance while ensuring strong and solid foundations.


Engineering departement

Our internal engineering department enables us to provide accurate, expert analysis for all types of project. Thanks to their specialist knowledge of helical pile foundations, our engineers can provide reliable and adapted technical recommendations. This expertise is another proof of our commitment to our customers and our ability to guarantee fully integrated solutions, from beginning to end of the project.


Building code compliant

Our solution fully complies with current construction industry standards, ensuring regulatory compliance. Our certifications BBA, CE, ISO 9001, and UKCA, reflect our constant commitment to quality, safety, and the reliability of our products.
